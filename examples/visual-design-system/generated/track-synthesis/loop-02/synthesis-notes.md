# Synthesis 02 — measured resilience repair

## Parent contributions retained

- **Editorial-02:** title-first hierarchy, explicit labels, rule-led alignment, accessibility discipline, and a real variant/token contract remain foundational. Opus and Terra preferred this whole-brief completeness; that evidence is still accepted.
- **Generative-02:** the dedicated routing module remains the ownable Loop Lab signature. Gemini's dissent—that this visual language is more memorable and digitally native than editorial print mimicry—is preserved in the final visual direction.
- **Synthesis-01:** protected 72px structural gutter, dark contrast-safe CTA, explicit metadata semantics, and the combined serif/editorial plus signal-path composition are retained.

## Rejections and repairs

- **Synthesis-01 fixed compact metadata columns:** rejected after direct render evidence. `HUB A + WEB` ended at x=779.358, 3.358px beyond the card. Loop 02 gives flexible venues a full-width row; the longer `INNOVATION HUB + ONLINE` now has 82.761px spare before the inner edge.
- **Fixed title scale for every content density:** rejected. The system now has named standard and dense tiers rather than arbitrary per-card shrinkage.
- **Fixed badge widths:** rejected as a system rule. Width is now a measured-label-plus-padding clamp, with explicit overflow behavior.
- **Parent generative CTA and unsafe title boundary:** remain rejected. White on `#173B57` measures 11.67:1, and the rendered dense title retains 111.516px to the module.
- **Empty icon placeholders and palette-only tokens:** remain rejected in favor of explicit labels and production-minded component/layout/state contracts.

## Preserved dissent

- Gemini preferred generative-02 for pure visual impact and warned against generic editorial mimicry.
- Opus and Terra preferred editorial-02 because primary hierarchy, clarity, objective rigor, and reuse were stronger.
- This synthesis deliberately keeps those views in tension: visual identity comes from the modular routing panel; behavior and reading order come from editorial discipline. No self-promotion or independent champion claim is made.

## Loop 02 strengths

- Repairs a visible Loop 01 component failure with rendered measurements, not a content substitution.
- Proves a three-line title, long venue, and long delivery badge together.
- Actual rendered title-to-panel separation is 111.516px, above the 64px gate.
- CTA normal-text contrast remains 11.67:1.
- Three variants share components while changing title density, venue length, delivery state, accent, capacity, and routing pattern.

## Remaining limitations

- The system is stress-tested for English strings only; translated expansion, non-Latin scripts, and right-to-left ordering remain untested.
- SVG `<text>` still depends on available system font fallbacks, so exact glyph widths vary by environment even though Inkscape evidence passes with wide margins.
- Visual quality scores are provisional. Per the independent-judge gate, synthesis-02 remains `needs_human_review` until a separate panel inspects the evidence.
