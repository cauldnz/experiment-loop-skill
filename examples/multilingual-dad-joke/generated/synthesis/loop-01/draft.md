# Synthesis Loop 01 — Walls Meet at the Corner

## English
**Setup:** What did one wall say to the wall next to it?  
**Punchline:** Let’s meet at the corner!

## Français
**Question :** Qu’est-ce qu’un mur a dit au mur d’à côté ?  
**Réponse :** On se retrouve au coin !

## Español
**Pregunta:** ¿Qué le dijo una pared a la de al lado?  
**Remate:** ¡Nos vemos en el rincón!

## 日本語
**問い:** 壁が隣の壁に何て言った？  
**答え:** 角で会おう！

The mechanism is the same in every version: adjacent walls arrange to meet at the corner where they already physically join.
