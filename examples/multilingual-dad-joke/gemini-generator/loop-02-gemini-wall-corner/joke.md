# Meet at the corner

## Concept

Anthropomorphized walls meet at a corner, which is both architectural geometry and a meeting place.

## English

What did one wall say to the other wall? I'll meet you at the corner.

## French

Que dit un mur à un autre mur ? « On se retrouve au coin. »

## Spanish

¿Qué le dice una pared a otra pared? « Nos vemos en la esquina. »

## Japanese

ひとつの壁がもうひとつの壁に何と言った？「角で待ち合わせよう。」
